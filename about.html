ABOUT US

F1 Motors, a pre-owned sport car dealer deals with popular sport brands. Before we get ahead of ourselves, we want to welcome you to F1 Motors. While nothing can replace thing on-the-lot experience. We appreciate you taking the time today to visit our web site.

F1 Motors started in 2009 as a benchmark model for the Pre-Used, or how we prefer to see it as, PreLoved Car Brand. The mission was simple, direct and drove effect - delivering a new dimension of sport while standardising & raising platforms for the used car market in all over the world.


F1 Motors caters Exclusive Cars to all kinds of clients, whether it be a car collector or a casual user. First motors sources the most highly picked cars for competitive prices, with our networks reaching beyond.

F1 Motors is a sport used car dealership located in the Hollywood Hills, LA,California-90068 . It is one of the select few dealerships in California that specializes in the purchase and sale of high-end pre-owned British and German sport cars along with other select few car brands.

F1 Motors was established with the purpose of serving used car buyers and sellers with the best-in-class deals and service. A few popular used sport car brands F1 Motors deals in include Audi, BMW, MercedesBenz, GMC, Jaguar, Ford, Maserati, Volkswagen, Volvo and many more. We buy and sell a broad selection of car types which include Hatchback, Sedan, SUV/Crossovers, Coupe and Convertibles sport cars.
